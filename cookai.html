Features

Everything a real
kitchen needs

Built with structured context injection so every answer is grounded in exactly where you are in the recipe.

🎙️
Hands-free voice control
Speak naturally. Say "next step", "repeat that", or ask any cooking question without touching a screen.
🧠
Step-level context memory
The assistant always knows which step you're on, what came before, and all your ingredients — automatically.
🔄
Smart substitutions
No butter? Ask and get a context-aware substitute that fits your current recipe and dietary needs.
⚡
Instant navigation
Navigation commands like "go back" are handled in milliseconds — no LLM call, no lag.
🛡️
Safety logic built-in
Warnings fire automatically for overheating, unsafe temperatures, and skipped critical steps.
📚
50+ recipe knowledge base
Recipes from MealDB API, structured into indexed steps for instant retrieval and context injection.
